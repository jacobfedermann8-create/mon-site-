About AKIMBO INVESTISSEMENT SA

Founded in 2005 and based in Geneva, AKIMBO INVESTISSEMENT SA specializes in comprehensive asset management, capital investment, and financial structuring for both private and institutional clients. With over two decades of experience, we provide bespoke strategies that align with our clients’ long-term objectives, ensuring growth, security, and sustainability across all investment classes.

Our dedicated team of experts leverages deep market insights, rigorous risk management, and tailored investment solutions to deliver exceptional results. From real estate portfolios and venture capital opportunities to sponsorship and strategic holdings, we help our clients navigate the complexities of global investments with confidence.

Our Core Services

We provide highly specialized services to meet the diverse needs of our clients:

  • Asset Management: Tailored portfolio management focused on long-term performance, capital preservation, and risk-adjusted growth.
  • Real Estate Investments: Curated Swiss and European properties, over 50 assets managed to maximize returns and diversification.
  • Startups & Venture Capital: Strategic investment in high-potential startups, over 25 ventures supported, offering guidance, capital, and growth expertise.
