About AKIMBO Investment SA

An independent Swiss investment firm built on long-term vision, discipline, and strategic capital allocation.

Our History

Founded in Switzerland, AKIMBO Investment SA has been operating as an independent investment and asset management firm since 2003.

The firm was established to meet the needs of investors seeking a professional, discreet, and long-term partner for the management and structuring of their assets.

Over the years, AKIMBO Investment SA has developed strong expertise across multiple asset classes, including real estate, private equity, startup investments, and strategic partnerships.

Our experience has been built through direct involvement in investment structuring, asset management, and long-term portfolio oversight across Switzerland and Europe.

Our Vision & Approach

A disciplined investment philosophy focused on sustainability and long-term value creation.

AKIMBO Investment SA follows a long-term investment approach grounded in discipline, selectivity, and capital preservation.

We do not pursue short-term speculation. Instead, we focus on carefully selected investment opportunities supported by strong fundamentals, prudent risk management, and clear strategic alignment.

Our independence allows us to remain fully aligned with our clients’ interests, ensuring transparency, objectivity, and governance at every stage of the investment process.

Our Core Values

Independence

Independent decision-making ensures objective investment selection aligned with client interests.

Discipline

A structured and rigorous approach to investment analysis, execution, and risk management.

Long-Term Vision

Focus on sustainable growth, capital preservation, and value creation over time.

Confidentiality

Absolute discretion and confidentiality in all client relationships and investment activities.
