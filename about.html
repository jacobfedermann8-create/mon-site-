About AKIMBO INVESTISSEMENT SA

Our Story

Founded in 2003

AKIMBO INVESTISSEMENT SA was founded in Geneva, Switzerland, with the vision to provide exceptional asset management and investment solutions for private and institutional clients.

Expansion & Growth

Over the years, the company expanded across Europe, diversifying its investments in real estate, startups, venture capital, and strategic sponsorships. Today, AKIMBO manages portfolios that include high-end residential and commercial properties, technology startups, and premium sponsorship opportunities.

Expertise & Leadership

With more than 20 years of experience, our team combines deep market knowledge, innovative strategies, and a client-first approach to deliver sustainable growth and capital preservation across multiple asset classes.

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is to provide tailored investment solutions that grow and protect wealth for our clients, maintaining the highest standards of transparency, professionalism, and integrity.

Our core values: Excellence, Innovation, Transparency, Client-focus, Sustainability.

Our Expertise

We specialize in Asset & Wealth Management, Real Estate Investments, Startups & Venture Capital, and Strategic Sponsorships. Each service is delivered with precision, professionalism, and long-term vision.

Asset & Wealth Management

Comprehensive portfolio management with allocation across equities, bonds, alternative funds, real estate, and startups.

Regular reporting, performance analysis, and strategic advice ensure capital preservation and growth.

Real Estate Investments

Projects analyzed for ROI, risk management, and long-term capital appreciation.

Startups & Venture Capital

Sponsorship & Strategic Partnerships
