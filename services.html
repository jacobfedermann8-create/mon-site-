Our Premium Investment Services

At AKIMBO INVESTISSEMENT SA, we provide tailored investment solutions to maximize growth and security. Explore our areas of expertise to understand where we invest and how we create value for our clients.

Asset Management

Our asset management services are designed to optimize and protect your financial portfolio. We develop tailored investment strategies, diversifying across markets, sectors, and geographies. Our approach combines Swiss precision, expert analysis, and continuous monitoring to maximize long-term returns while managing risks effectively.

Real Estate Investments

We invest in high-quality real estate assets in Switzerland and selected international markets. Our focus includes commercial, residential, and luxury properties that offer stable returns and capital appreciation. Each project is evaluated for location, potential growth, and risk mitigation, ensuring long-term value for our investors.

Startup & Venture Capital

We actively support innovative startups and venture capital projects with high growth potential. Our investment strategy targets technology, fintech, healthtech, and other emerging industries. We provide not only capital but also strategic guidance, networking, and mentorship to accelerate growth and maximize investor returns.

Sponsorship & Strategic Partnerships

We offer sponsorships and strategic partnerships that create long-term value and visibility for our clients. Collaborating with selected businesses and events, we maximize brand impact and strengthen market positioning. Each partnership is carefully chosen to align with our clients’ objectives and investment strategy.

Financial Advisory & Project Structuring

Our financial advisory services provide end-to-end support for capital allocation, project structuring, and investment strategy. We help clients navigate complex financial landscapes with expert insights, risk analysis, and strategic planning. Our goal is to ensure optimal execution, maximize returns, and secure sustainable growth.
